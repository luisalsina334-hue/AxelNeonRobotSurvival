Welcome to Newlink: Axel Robot!

HOW TO PLAY:
1. Double-click 'index.html' to open the game in your web browser.
2. Press F11 to go Fullscreen for the best experience.

CONTROLS:
- Move: W, A, S, D
- Aim: Mouse
- Shoot: Left Click

OBJECTIVE:
Defeat the enemy waves to advance through 5 levels of increasing difficulty.
